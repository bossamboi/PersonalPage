About

Hi, I'm Eric! I was born and raised in sunny southern California, then spent some time in Nashville, TN where I graduated from Vanderbilt University with a B.E in Mechanical Engineering and succumbed to enjoying country music. In my free time, you can find me cheffing in the kitchen, shredding up a mountain, or stuck in a bunker at the nearest golf course.

Prior to software, I worked in the construction industry as a virtual design engineer developing and coordinating 3D building models for mechanical, electrical, and low voltage systems in labs and hospitals. I've always loved engineering for its iterative processes. I hope to continue to build better and smarter products in the software space that enhance people's lives.
